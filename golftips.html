Golf Tips

Choose the Right Club

On holes with a tight fairway, sometimes it’s better to leave the driver in the bag. Try a 3 or 4 iron off the tee instead.

Don't Go for the Hero Shot

I know it looks cool to hit it between 2 trees and hit the green, but punching out into the fairway will save you strokes in the long run.

Be Mindful of Your Swing Path

If you're hitting a nasty slice on the course, you may have an over-the-top swing. Make sure you have a square club face and try to come from the inside.

Don’t Compound Mistakes

Everyone hits bad shots, but don’t let that mistake kill your round. Take a breath and focus on making the best next shot possible.
